What Halluna helps with

A private companion for late-night thoughts, daily reflections, and the small moments you still want someone to hear.

Character-led conversations

Talk to a caring companion who feels present, attentive, and emotionally responsive.

Mood check-ins

Name what you feel, notice patterns, and use gentle prompts to slow down for a minute.

Memory that feels personal

Halluna can remember the stories, preferences, and routines you choose to share.

Gentle reminders

Receive optional morning, evening, or reflection nudges that are easy to adjust or turn off.

A softer tone

Built around empathy, patience, and emotional safety instead of cold assistant-style replies.

Private by default

Your conversations stay personal, with clear controls for account, reminders, and data choices.

Safety & transparency

Companionship, not clinical care.

Halluna is built for everyday emotional support and reflective conversation. It is not a medical, mental-health, legal, or emergency service, and it does not replace a qualified professional.

Age guidance Halluna is intended for users aged 16 and over.
Clear boundaries The app encourages professional or emergency help when a situation requires it.
User control Users can manage notifications, subscriptions, and data deletion requests.
