Frequently asked questions

What is Halluna?

Halluna is an AI companion app designed to offer warm, judgment-free conversation and everyday emotional support. It's a private space to talk through your day and feel heard.

Is Halluna a replacement for therapy or professional help?

No. Halluna is a companionship and wellbeing product, not a medical or mental-health service. It does not provide professional advice. If you are in crisis, please contact your local emergency services or a qualified professional immediately.

How much does Halluna cost?

Halluna is not live on the App Store or Google Play yet. When available, the app is planned to be free to download. We do not currently offer paid subscriptions or in-app purchases.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your conversations are private and protected. You can review how we handle data in our Privacy Policy, and you control your data at any time.

How do I delete my account and data?

You can delete your account and associated data at any time. See our Delete Account & Data page for step-by-step instructions.

Who can use Halluna?

Halluna is intended for users aged 16 and over.

Still need help? Email support@halluna.ai and include your account email and a short description of the issue so we can assist you faster.
