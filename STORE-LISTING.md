## Full description (App Store & Google Play)

Halluna is a caring AI companion app designed for women — a warm, judgment-free space to talk, reflect, and feel genuinely heard whenever you need it.

Life gets heavy. Some days you just want someone to listen without judging, advising, or rushing you. Halluna is built for exactly those moments.

WHY YOU'LL LOVE HALLUNA

• Always available — Chat any time of day or night. No waiting, no awkwardness.
• Judgment-free — Share whatever's on your mind. Halluna listens with patience and warmth.
• Emotional wellbeing — Gentle reflection prompts and encouragement to help you feel a little lighter.
• Personalized — Halluna remembers what matters to you and adapts over time.
• Private by design — Your conversations are yours. Manage and delete your data anytime.
• Daily check-ins — Soft reminders to pause, breathe, and reconnect with yourself.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Download and create your private account.
2. Start a conversation — say hello, vent about your day, or ask for encouragement.
3. Come back whenever you like. Halluna grows alongside you.

A GENTLE NOTE

Halluna is a companionship and emotional-wellbeing app. It is not a medical, mental-health, or emergency service, and does not provide professional advice. If you are in crisis, please contact your local emergency services or a qualified professional.

Halluna is intended for users aged 16 and over.

Download Halluna today — because you deserve to feel heard.
